Step 5 — Risk Awareness
Common mistakes that cost seats

Most seat losses are not due to low scores — they happen due to avoidable strategy errors.

⚠️
Wrong Preference Order
Filling choices without cutoff strategy leads to losing a better college you could have gotten in Round 2.
↗ Affects 1 in 3 students
⏰
Missing Deadlines
NEET counselling has strict day-level deadlines. Missing even one step can disqualify the entire round.
↗ Often non-recoverable
💸
Security Deposit Loss
Withdrawing after allotment without proper resignation leads to full deposit forfeiture — often ₹50,000+.
↗ Avg. loss: ₹50,000+
📋
Bond Rule Confusion
Many private colleges have rural service bonds. Not understanding these creates long-term obligations.
↗ Legally binding
🔄
AIQ Resignation Error
Resigning from AIQ without following MCC's exact protocol permanently blocks re-entry in all rounds.
↗ Blocks re-entry forever
📁
Document Errors
Caste validity, EWS, domicile — each has state-specific and central versions. Wrong one = seat cancelled.
↗ Seat cancelled at reporting
