# ASMI Career — Product Context

## What it is
Maharashtra's #1 NEET medical admissions counselling company. Helps students and families navigate the complex process of getting into MBBS, BDS, BAMS, and BHMS colleges across India.

## Users
- NEET students (17–22) and their parents, primarily in Maharashtra
- Anxious, high-stakes decision makers who need clarity and trust
- Often comparing multiple counselling services

## Brand
- Name: ASMI Career — Youth Career Advisor
- Primary: Purple #7b41b3
- Accent: Gold #ffd700
- Tone: Authoritative, honest, reassuring. Not sales-y. Not clinical.
- 11+ years | 25,000+ admissions | 6 branches in Maharashtra

## Register
brand — marketing/landing site. Design IS the product.

## Anti-references
- Traditional consultancy look (dark purple everywhere, stock photos of students)
- Generic AI slop: centered 3-card grids, purple gradients on white, flat animations, Inter everywhere
- Hospital/clinic aesthetic
- Overly corporate

## Strategic principles
- Trust first: every design choice must reinforce credibility
- Clarity over cleverness: confused visitors don't convert
- Product-feel: inspired by zynerd.com — clean, modern, data-driven, app-like
- Gold CTAs only: #ffd700 with purple/dark text, never used decoratively
