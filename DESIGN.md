# ASMI Career — Design System

## Color strategy: Committed
One saturated purple carries the brand. Gold is strictly functional (CTAs, callouts). Dark surfaces for hero sections.

## Colors
- Brand purple: #7b41b3
- Brand dark: #5a2d8a
- Gold (CTA only): #ffd700
- Gold text: #5a4400
- Dark bg: #09090f
- Body bg: #f6f5f9
- White: #ffffff
- Text primary: #0f0e17
- Text secondary: #4a4760
- Text muted: #9490a8

## Typography
- Display/headlines: Syne 700/800 — geometric, bold, product-feel
- Body: Inter 400/500/600/700
- Display tracking: -0.03em on large sizes
- Body line-height: 1.6–1.7

## Elevation / surfaces
1. Dark hero: #09090f with purple radial glow
2. White sections: #ffffff
3. Off-white sections: #f6f5f9
4. Cards: #ffffff with 1px rgba(0,0,0,0.07) border

## Motion
- Scroll reveals: opacity 0→1 + translateY 24px→0, 0.6s ease, threshold 0.08
- Hover: translateY(-4px) with shadow on cards
- CTA shimmer on hover (linear gradient sweep)
- No bounce, no elastic, no layout animation

## Components
- btn-gold: #ffd700 bg, #5a4400 text, 12px radius, shimmer on hover
- section-label: 0.7rem, 700, uppercase, 0.1em tracking, #7b41b3
- tool-card: white bg, 20px radius, hover lift + purple shadow
- branch-card: white bg, 16px radius, hover purple border
- testi-card: white bg, 20px radius, large quote mark watermark
