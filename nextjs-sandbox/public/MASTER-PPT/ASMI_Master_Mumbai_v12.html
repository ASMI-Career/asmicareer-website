🔑 Status Retention — Key Concept
The Status Retention Form is unique to Maharashtra. It determines whether your current admission "locks" you out of further rounds or keeps you eligible for upgradation.

Don't fill it = stay in the race.
Fill it with YES = you're done.
ℹ️ No Security Deposit
Maharashtra does not require a security deposit like MCC. Exiting in Round 1 (Free Exit) carries no financial penalty.
