● Mandatory — everyone needs this ● Conditional — based on category ● Originals required at reporting

1. Academic Documents

Class 10 Marksheet & Certificate
Required for date of birth proof. Board original + 2 attested copies.
Mandatory Original needed
Class 12 Marksheet
Physics, Chemistry, Biology marks must show 50%+ (40% for SC/ST). Original + 3 copies.
Mandatory Original needed
Class 12 Passing Certificate
Separate from marksheet. Issued by your state board.
Mandatory
NEET 2025 Scorecard
Download from nta.ac.in. Print 3 copies. Keep roll number handy.
Mandatory Original needed
NEET Admit Card 2025
Some states require this at document verification. Print and keep.
Mandatory

2. Identity & Address Proof

Aadhaar Card
Student's own Aadhaar. Original + 3 self-attested copies.
Mandatory Original needed
Passport-size Photographs
At least 10 recent photos. White background, formal dress. Same as NEET application photo if possible.
Mandatory
Domicile / Residence Certificate
Required for state quota. Must be issued by Tehsildar or District Collector. Maharashtra students — check validity date.
Mandatory for state quota Original needed

3. Category Certificates

Caste Certificate (OBC / SC / ST / SEBC / VJ-NT)
Must be in student's name. Issued by SDO/Tahsildar. Central and State versions differ — get both if possible.
If applicable Original needed
Caste Validity Certificate
Separate from caste certificate. Issued by Scrutiny Committee. Allow 4–6 weeks to obtain. Critical — without this, category benefit is denied.
SC / ST / OBC / SEBC / VJ-NT Original needed
Non-Creamy Layer Certificate (NCL)
Required for OBC, SEBC, VJ-NT. Must be current year. Issued by Tehsildar.
OBC / SEBC / VJ-NT
EWS Certificate
Income & asset certificate issued by Tehsildar. Must be for current financial year. Used for 10% EWS quota.
EWS category only Original needed

4. Registration & Online Documents

MCC Registration Confirmation (AIQ)
Printout of your registered application on mcc.nic.in for All India Quota counselling.
AIQ / Deemed / Central
State Counselling Registration Printout
DMER Maharashtra (cetcell.mahacet.org) or equivalent state authority printout.
State quota
Allotment Letter
Issued after each round. Print immediately. Take to college at time of reporting.
Mandatory at reporting Original needed
Fee Payment Receipt
Security deposit / seat acceptance fee payment confirmation. Keep both digital and printed copies.
Mandatory

5. Medical Fitness

Medical Fitness Certificate
Signed by a registered MBBS doctor. States you are physically and mentally fit for a medical course. Some colleges have their own format.
Mandatory at reporting
⚠️

Common mistakes that cost seats

  • Caste Validity Certificate missing — category benefit denied on the spot
  • NCL certificate is of previous year — rejected at verification
  • Domicile certificate has wrong address or expired date
  • Photos don't match NEET application photo
  • Allotment letter not printed before reporting deadline
