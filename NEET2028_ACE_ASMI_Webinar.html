CBT
The Biggest Shift in NEET History
Pen & Paper → Computer.
What Changes for You?
Before — PBT
Pen & Paper Mode
  • OMR sheet, physical bubbling
  • One fixed question set, same for all
  • Handwriting errors can cost marks
  • No chance to review after submission
  • Results 4–6 weeks after exam
After — CBT
Computer Based Test
  • On-screen interface, click to answer
  • Multiple test sessions possible (different slots)
  • Mark for review & revisit anytime
  • Faster results processing
  • Requires comfort with computer interface
🖥️
Practice on Interface Early
CBT mock tests will be different from JEE mock tests. Students need specific NEET CBT practice — don't assume JEE prep covers it.
⏱️
Time Management Changes
Scrolling through 180 questions on screen vs. flipping paper — navigation speed matters more than ever.
🎯
Marking Scheme Unchanged
+4 for correct, -1 for wrong. Total 720 marks. Only the delivery medium changes, not the exam structure.
📍
More Test Centres Expected
CBT infrastructure allows NTA to add more centres, potentially reducing travel burden for students in smaller cities.

After NEET Result
The Complete Admission Process
1
NEET Score & Rank Declaration
NTA releases All India Rank (AIR). Your rank determines which quota & colleges you can apply for.
2
All India Quota (AIQ) Counselling — MCC
Medical Counselling Committee runs online counselling for 15% AIQ seats in Government colleges + 100% Deemed + Central universities.
3
State Quota Counselling — CET Cell Maharashtra
85% State quota seats via Maharashtra CET Cell. Separate CAP rounds for State, Minority & NRI quota.
4
Deemed & Private Colleges — Separate Process
Many Deemed universities conduct their own counselling. Cutoffs, fees, and seat availability vary widely.
5
Document Verification & Admission Confirmation
Original documents verified at college. Fee payment confirms seat. Missing deadlines means seat cancellation.

  • Top 500–1000 AIR required

⚠️ Critical Warning
Admission Frauds Are Real.
Protect Your Family.
🚫
"Guaranteed seat" promises — No one outside official MCC/CET Cell can guarantee a seat. Anyone claiming so is a fraud.
💰
Cash payments outside receipts — Always demand official fee receipts from the college/authority. Cash "donations" are illegal under MCI/NMC rules.
📋
Fake counselling portals — Fake websites mimicking MCC, CET Cell or state portals. Always verify URLs carefully before logging in.
🧾
Agents with "direct contacts" — No middleman has insider access to government seat allocation. This is a scam every time.
⚠️
One wrong decision can cost your family ₹5–25 Lakhs and a full academic year. Verify everything through official channels only.
✅
Always use: mcc.nic.in for AIQ · cetcell.mahacet.org for Maharashtra · Official college websites only
